About SchedUREC

SchedUREC is a student-built platform designed to help JMU students stay on top of group fitness classes offered by UREC. With this app, you can view the latest class schedules, save the ones you want to attend, and even sync them to your Apple Calendar with helpful reminders.

Our goal is to make your UREC experience stress-free, so you can focus more on your health and less on missing registration deadlines. 💪

Whether you're into yoga, HIIT, cycling, or dance — SchedUREC keeps you connected and organized!
